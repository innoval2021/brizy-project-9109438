What Zia Teaching Academy does?

Zia Teaching Academy specializes in the development and training of quality ESL educators by providing them with a unique syllabus compiled by experts in English language training. Our company prides itself in providing specialized training and support virtually and onsite that will effectively equip future teachers to perform exceptional teaching in the classroom, without the added stress of adapting to a new environment. We take away the stress of training new teachers from the educational institutes and allow them to create a more efficient and positive classroom and working environment. 

What makes us different?

Clients will find our syllabus and training differs from other TEFL companies as it goes in depth in theory, provides a platform to practice teaching techniques, and provides helpful materials and resources within one TEFL course that is affordable to our clients. We also provide continuous professional development webinar training and job support services such as CV compilation, interview preparation and application assistance that will further equip our clients and give them an advantage in their job applications. Our TEFL training program has been evaluated by teachers and students in various countries who believe we have revolutionized the field of ESL teacher training.

Our TEFL Program

Our TEFL program is only one of the amazing training programs that we offer. We have a dedicated and qualified team who are equipped to revolutionize the corporate world. For this purpose, we are offering specialized English training programs to businesses who have employees that have difficulty with the English language. We train employees in business English in speaking, customer support services as well as document compilation. We strongly believe in creating a positive environment in both educational institutes as well as professional environments for which we also offer team building and corporate wellness exercises virtually and on site, which is needed in the current pandemic. â€‹
