HOW LONG WILL IT TAKE TO COMPLETE?


The simple answer is in 3 modules, but since you are in control of your learning, time management and progress you can take as much time as you like. We at Zia Teaching Academy understand that all learners progress at different speeds so we do not by any means put a time limit on when you should complete the course by. We do however encourage our learners to work diligently and put some time management into effect.

The three modules you will have to complete will deal with the below mentioned topics and many other important aspects.In Module 1 you will learn about teaching Abroad and teaching adults versus teaching young students.
